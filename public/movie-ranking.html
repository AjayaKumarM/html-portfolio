The Best Movies According to Ajay

My top 3 movies of all-time

Spirited away

I watched this movie randomly on a Sunday afternoon and loved it throughout. Watched it the second time and also loved it the second watch.

Kaidhi

This movies happens in one night a simple storyline but an extraordinary screenplay. Racy film gets better after every rewatch. Checkov's gun is perfectly executed.

Spiderman 2

In my childhood i watched this movie and loved it especially when Peter Parker ditches his suit i felt sad and when he returned with that famous and iconic "He's Back" scene with the beautiful and fitting orchestra damn i had tears and goosebumps.
